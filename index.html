About Us

Founded by Antonio Willis Jr. in Louisville, Kentucky, AllWayze Elevate is a community-based program dedicated to teaching youth the life skills they need to survive and thrive — the ones school never taught them. We specialize in mentoring boys and young men aged 14-24 through hands-on workshops and personal development.

Our Program

AllWayze Elevate focuses on real-world life skills including:

  • Managing emotions and expressing feelings without violence
  • Building and protecting credit
  • Opening a business or LLC
  • Effective communication and respectful listening
  • Building healthy relationships and setting boundaries
  • Resisting peer pressure and avoiding toxic cycles

Our 12-Month Goals

  • Reach 100+ youth through events and workshops
  • Graduate 30–50 youth from full 6-week programs
  • Match 25+ youth with mentors
  • Help launch 10+ youth businesses or job placements
  • Partner with 5+ schools or local organizations
